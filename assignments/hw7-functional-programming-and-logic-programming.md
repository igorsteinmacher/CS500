Initially, all the disks are stacked in decreasing sizes of diameter (i.e., the smallest disk is placed on the top) and they are all on rod A. To solve the puzzle, one has to move the entire stack to another rod, following these rules:

1. Only one disk can be moved at a time.
2. Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack
3. No disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
