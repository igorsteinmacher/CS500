# Topic: Grammar Ambiguity and EBNF

**(Nov 21)**

Following up on our last topic, we will discuss ambiguity in programming languages. Ambiguity may cause problems since the grammar allows different derivations for a single word or sentence. In this lecture, we will explore potential issues caused by ambiguity and how programming languages avoid them. We wrap up by presenting the Extended BNF notation to facilitate the creation of programming languages grammars.
