# Topic: Grammar

**(Nov-14/Nov-16)**

The topic will bring the concept of grammars and their role in a programming language. We will use a practical, example-based approach to understand how grammars are used to recognize sentences in a programming language. We will use pieces of grammars used by programming languages to derive a few sentences.
