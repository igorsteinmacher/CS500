# Topic: Names and Scope

**(Nov 28)**

In this topic, the focus will be on how variables are manipulated by programming languages. We will discuss typing and names, followed by a more in-depth understanding of how addresses are manipulated. We will also explore the different strategies used to manage scope: static and dynamic scoping.
